WELCOME TO ILIGAN CITY!

Known to be The City of Waterfalls but this humble city has more than majestic waterfalls and cold pools to offer. Iligan is a place unlike any other. It is a highly progressive city perfect for food trips, socializing and adventures. A perfect mix of chill and excitement. Iligan is definitely not for the faint of heart!
